About Mini

Mini is a sweet child full of mischief and energy. Most would describe her as a little ball of energy. She spent the first 7 months with her Mommy where she developed her interest in Rubik's cubes. After she went to accompany her Daddy, she gained an interest in video games. Alas, this gave her Mommy a headache because she would catch Mini neglecting her sleep to play games (due to her Daddy's encouragement). This sparks up her rebellious nature that is similar to her older brother, Alex. Although she is mischievous, she adores her little brother, Finn, whom she calls and chat twice a week.
