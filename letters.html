To My Sun and Stars

Feb 14, 2025


Happy Valentine's Day! By the time you're reading this, I have completed this, but right now, I am less than 20% done. I know this letter was very short so I hope my love for you will be conveyed in the upcoming testimony.

Would you be surprised that you are the first person that I officially spent Valentine's with? It is also my first time asking someone to be my Valentine. Maybe not because I told you I wasn't with anyone officially before you.

Since my last letter, I've learned a lot about you and you learned a lot about me. We spent a night talking about how we would find each other in every universe. I don't know if you knew but I was bawling my eyes out

because I did not want to imagine a universe where we didn't find each other. That's when you reassured me and said we would find each other.

I really want to build a statue in remembrance of us when we leave this Earth, so that future generations would look at the statue in envy and talk amongst themselves how we loved each other so deeply. When we cease to exist, our love would still be alive, flourishing and abundant.

Right now, you're progressing through college and I cannot express how happy I am for you. I remember the day when you got into your car after talking to your advisor, smiling from ear to ear and thanking me a lot for pushing you forward. In reality, you were the one who

pushed yourself, I was just in the sidelines cheering you on. I am so so proud of you for taking that step even when you know you're afraid.

On this day of love and every future day beyond us, I am glad that I met you. You changed me in ways where I didn't even know I could change. You already knew I was never the type of person to coyly call someone by pet names. However, with you, I love to call you that until my breath stops. I never felt so safe and comfortable with a person before I met you. You are deserving of all the love from me. I love you Babo. Our kids will grow up in a home where their parents will always be teenagers in love. Thank you for being yourself and being here with me.

Love, your soulmate
